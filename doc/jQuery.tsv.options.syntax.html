jQuery.tsv.options. syntax

A syntax consists of, at a minimum, a lexer and a parser kernel, for the parsing, and a valueSeparator and rowSeparator for the standard formatting kernel. More complex formats may require additional features. For example, CSV will require its own formatting kernel, to handle quotation.
